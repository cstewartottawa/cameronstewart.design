Establishing a Brand.

Working with a client who is enthusiastic about their brand is always rewarding. The aim of this project was to create a logo and secondary brand elements for a new gym, 613 Lift Ltd., in Ottawa, Ontario.

The Design Process.

Here I will talk about the process I went through designing the logo, perghaps referring to the sketches on the right of this paragraph. Initially I experimented with the idea of using a weight lifters body and the bar to create the shapes for the logo, after exploring a variety of ideas like this and discussing the client's vision, I decided that a simpler design was the better solution. The curved bar makes it feel like the weight is heavy and being lifted not resting stagnant on the floor. It aslos serves to lead the viewer's eye up through the logo, conveying the feeling that it is somehtign to be looked up at and admired.

Strength Through Consistency.

Incorporating the red bar element from the logo into other areas of the brand was a way of putting the 613 Lift stamp on components that typically go unnoticed, this is seen below on both the washrroms signs and a kettlebell mock-up.. I applied the bar from the main logo to the male and female changeroom signs.
