Establishing a Brand.

Working with a client who is enthusiastic about their brand is always a rewarding process. This project was to create a logo and some secondary brand elements for a new gym that recently opened in Ottawa, Ontario.

Strength Through Consistency.

Incorporating elements from the logo into other areas of the brand was a way of putting the 613 Lift stamp on components that typically go unnoticed. I applied the bar from the main logo to the male and female changeroom signs.
